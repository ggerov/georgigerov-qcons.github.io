⚡ My Services

1. Quality Management System (QMS) Development & Optimization

Services Offered:

  • Design, implementation, and global harmonization of QMS aligned with ICH GCP, ISO 9001, and regional regulatory frameworks.
  • Streamlining documentation control, Serious Breach, CAPA, change management, and deviation management systems.
  • Development and revision of corporate SOP frameworks and quality governance models.

Benefits for Clients: Harmonized, compliant, and agile QMS structure supporting business scalability. Reduced operational complexity and enhanced alignment and efficiency across regions. Improved audit and inspection readiness through process transparency and traceability.

2. Inspection Readiness & Regulatory Compliance

Services Offered:

  • Comprehensive inspection readiness programs (FDA, EMA, MHRA, PMDA) including pre-inspection assessments and mock audits.
  • Coaching and training Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) for inspection interviews and evidence presentation.
  • Development of inspection playbooks, control room strategy, and post-inspection CAPA follow-up management.

Benefits for Clients: Maintained "inspection-ready every day" culture and confidence in facing regulatory authorities. Reduced risk of critical findings through proactive preparation. Strengthened organizational capability and consistency in inspection performance.

3. Clinical Quality Assurance & GCP Oversight

Services Offered:

  • Development of GCP oversight frameworks across sponsors, CROs, and investigator sites.
  • Conduct of GCP audits (system, process, vendor, investigator site, and study-specific).
  • Quality governance across Clinical Operations, Medical Affairs, and Pharmacovigilance.

Benefits for Clients: Enhanced GCP compliance and data integrity across the clinical trial lifecycle. Reduced risk exposure through proactive quality oversight. Increased Clients' confidence and successful regulatory submission support.

4. External Service Providers & CRO Quality Oversight

Services Offered:

  • Qualification, auditing, and ongoing performance monitoring of External Service Providers and CROs.
  • Development of Quality Agreements and governance mechanisms for outsourced activities.
  • Oversight of technology service providers, including validated IT systems and data integrity controls.

Benefits for Clients: Strengthened partnerships through clear quality expectations and accountability. Continuous performance improvement and compliance transparency. Risk-based External Service Providers management aligned with business strategy and regulatory requirements.

5. Quality Training, Capability Building & Organizational Development

Services Offered:

  • Design and delivery of bespoke QA/QC and GxP training programs, including regulatory expectations, audit skills, and inspection readiness.
  • Development of competency matrices and continuous learning frameworks.

Benefits for Clients: Empowered teams with consistent quality knowledge and inspection confidence. Improved quality ownership across functions and reduced dependency on reactive quality management. Long-term sustainability of quality culture and leadership development.

6. Quality Risk Management, Serious Breach Management & Continuous Improvement

Services Offered:

  • Implementation of ICH Q9(R1)-based risk management frameworks to proactively identify, assess, and mitigate compliance and operational risks.
  • Serious Breach Management: coordination of assessment, investigation, reporting, and CAPA oversight, ensuring regulatory compliance and data integrity.
  • Development and implementation of Key Quality Indicators (KQIs) and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to monitor compliance, process performance, and inspection readiness.
  • Design and deployment of Quality Dashboards, providing real-time visibility into compliance trends, quality metrics, and audit outcomes.
  • Facilitation of root cause analysis and CAPA effectiveness checks, embedding lessons learned across global teams.
  • Leadership of continuous improvement initiatives using Lean Six Sigma and risk-based quality analytics.

Benefits for Clients: Proactive detection and mitigation of serious compliance risks and process deviations. Evidence-based decision-making through transparent and data-driven performance metrics. Enhanced cross-functional collaboration and alignment on quality objectives. Strengthened regulatory confidence through demonstrable control and continuous improvement culture.
